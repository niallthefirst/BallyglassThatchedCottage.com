Ballyglass cottage is an authentic Irish Thatched Cottage.

Originally built by the Fallon famiily in the 1850s, approximately 150yrs old.

The cottage been beautifully restored by the same Fallon family in 2005.

This is a Luxury cottage for holiday rental in Ireland. Book through Airbnb.

View on Airbnb Ballyglass Thatched Cottage Heart of Ireland

The cottage consists of 3 bedrooms; 1 double and 2 twins. The twin rooms are what is described as loft rooms i.e. upstairs. The cottage is designed with a wooden vaulted ceiling. Sleeps 6. Pet friendly.

Whether its just a short weekend refresh or a peaceful extended stay amongst the beautiful natural surrounds you will come away inspired and revitalised - we guarantee it!

There is no quiter location in Ireland, the cottage is located in a small valley, the accessing road is shared with only a handful of other homes so the area is very very quiet.

The cottage is available for holiday rental, situated in the centre of Ireland. The cottage was restored as luxury home and now serves as a holiday home for rent.

Just over 1.5 hour from Dublin city and 1.5 hour from Galway city yet in the nestled in the middle of quiet countryside.

Ideal as a family getaway or a central base to see the rest of the country.

This beautifully restored Irish thatch cottage is now available for your perfect country get away. Described as quirky it has some very unique characteristics as well as having all mod-cons you'd expect in a modern home.

The cottage was orignally built in the 19th century, probably post famine, by the Fallon family. The great great grandson of the original builders, Ray Fallon, now deceased restored the cottage with great care in 2005.

I am the son of Ray Fallon, Niall. The cottage and farm are still with the same Fallon family.

The cottage also has large garden with orchard.

Additionally the cottage is located on the grounds of a 50acre farm.
